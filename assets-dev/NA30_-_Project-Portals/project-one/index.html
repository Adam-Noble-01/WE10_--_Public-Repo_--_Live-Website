Project One

Residential complex with sustainable design

Project Overview

This residential complex was designed with sustainability at its core. The building incorporates passive solar design, rainwater harvesting, and energy-efficient systems throughout.

Located in an urban setting, the complex provides a green oasis for residents while maximizing space efficiency and natural light.

Key Features

  • 48 residential units ranging from studios to 3-bedroom apartments
  • Rooftop garden and community spaces
  • Ground floor commercial units
  • LEED Platinum certification
  • Community-focused amenities

Completed in 2022
